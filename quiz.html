Before taking the diagnostic quiz, you must decide whether you want to build the Brain or the Body of technology.

💻 Software Domain

What is it?
Software engineers write the code, logic, and algorithms that instruct computers what to do. They build the unseen intelligence behind screens.

What you will build:
Mobile apps, 3D video games, Artificial Intelligence models, massive databases, cloud server architectures, and cyber-security defense networks.

Choose this if:
You love solving mental logic puzzles, writing elegant code, analyzing data on a screen, and teaching machines how to think without touching physical tools.

⚙️ Hardware Domain

What is it?
Hardware engineers design, physically build, and test the microchips, electrical circuits, and mechanical systems that actually power technology.

What you will build:
Robots, micro-controllers, smart home sensors, advanced computer processors, automated drones, and global networking infrastructure.

Choose this if:
You love working with your hands, building physical machines, measuring electricity, and deeply understanding how computer components interact physically.
