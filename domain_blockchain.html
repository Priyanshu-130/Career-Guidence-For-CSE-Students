About the Domain

Building decentralized applications (DApps) on a shared, immutable ledger. Involves Smart Contracts, Tokenomics, and Cryptography.

Prerequisites

  • Cryptography: Hash functions (SHA-256), Public-Private Keys.
  • Smart Contracts: Solidity (Ethereum), Rust (Solana).
  • Web3 Libraries: Web3.js, Ethers.js.
  • Development: Hardhat, Truffle, Remix IDE.
  • Data Structures: Merkle Trees, Linked Lists.

Real-Life Applications

  • Finance: DeFi lending and borrowing w/o banks.
  • Supply Chain: Tracking products from origin to store.
  • Identity: Digital passports and voting systems.
  • NFTs: Digital art and ownership proof.
  • Smart Contracts: Automated legal agreements.

Learning Roadmap

Step 1: Blockchain Basics
Understand blocks, mining, consensus (PoW/PoS), and decentralization.
Step 2: Ethereum & Smart Contracts
Learn Solidity language. Write simple contracts on Remix IDE.
Step 3: Frontend Integration
Build a React app and connect it to the blockchain using Ethers.js.
Step 4: Tools & Testing
Master Hardhat for local development and testing contracts.
Step 5: Deployment
Deploy your DApp to a Testnet (Sepolia/Goerli) and then Mainnet.
