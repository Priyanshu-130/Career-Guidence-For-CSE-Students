Artificial Intelligence

About the Domain

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of making computers behave in ways that, until recently, we thought required human intelligence. It focuses on Problem Solving, Reasoning, Perception, and Robotics.

Learning Roadmap

Step 1: Foundations
Master Python and C++. Learn Discrete Mathematics (Logic, Sets, Graphs) and Probability.
Step 2: Problem Solving & Search
Learn Uninformed Search (BFS, DFS) and Informed Search (A*, Heuristics) algorithms.
Step 3: Game Theory
Implement Minimax algorithm, Alpha-Beta Pruning for games like Chess or Tic-Tac-Toe.
Step 4: Knowledge & Reasoning
Study Propositional Logic, First-Order Logic, and Inference Engines.
Step 5: Advanced AI Systems
Explore Probabilistic Reasoning (Bayesian Networks), Robotics (ROS), or Multi-Agent Systems.
