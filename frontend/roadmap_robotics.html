Robotics & Automation
Learning Roadmap & Resources

Follow this detailed step-by-step technical roadmap to build your expertise. We have designed 6 critical phases for you, alongside the highest quality learning materials available.

🛣️ Step-by-Step Learning Path

Step 1: Foundational Math & Physics

Build strong skills in Linear Algebra (matrices for transformations), Calculus, Kinematics, and Dynamics to understand spatial movement.

Step 2: Core Programming

Master C++ for high-performance robot control and Python for rapid prototyping and AI integration.

Step 3: Motors, Sensors & Controllers

Understand how to interface with Stepper motors, Lidar, cameras, IMUs. Learn electronics for hardware components.

Step 4: ROS (Robot Operating System)

Learn ROS/ROS2 framework. Understand nodes, topics, publishers/subscribers, and launch files to tie robotic software together.

Step 5: Control Theory & Path Planning

Study PID controllers, SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping), and algorithms like A* for autonomous navigation.

Step 6: Machine Vision & AI Integration

Implement OpenCV for visual reasoning and integrate Reinforcement Learning to build fully autonomous systems.
